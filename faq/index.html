Frequently Asked Questions

What is Moro?

Moro is a platform that lets users track how public opinion changes over time across different topics.

How are opinions measured?

Opinion metrics are based on user input and participation within the app.

Is Moro a prediction or financial app?

No. Moro does not offer financial trading or investment products.
